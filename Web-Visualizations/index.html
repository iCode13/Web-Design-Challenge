    • Summary: Weather vs Latitude

    The aim of the Lattitude project was to analyze how weather varies depending on a city's geographic location relative to the Equator. To accomplish this analysis, we first pulled data from the OpenWeatherMap API to assemble a dataset of over 500 cities from around the world.

    After assembling the dataset, we used Matplotlib to plot various measures of the weather vs. the city latitude. We looked at the following aspects of weather: maximum temperature, humidity, cloudiness, and wind speed. This site provides the source data and visualizations created as part of the analysis, as well as explanations and descriptions of any observed trends and correlations.
